The Cambrian Explosion

The Cambrian Explosion is an event that took place during the Cambrian period, a prehistoric time nearly 500 million years old. This period is known for the explosion of life after the Precambrian, the time after the Earth was formed. During this time, animals like the Haikouichthys and the Anomalocaris emerged. While the Cambrian isn't exactly an ideal place to live, it's an interesting time period that I am interested about.
